🏆 Stack Rank — Vibe + Stars

1 The Mooring ⭐ 4.6 Highest rated + most elevated waterfront experience. The dinner.
2 Midtown Oyster Bar ⭐ 4.3 The Midtown Loft is built for group celebrations. Rooftop + harbor = perfect.
3 Pasta Beach ⭐ 4.4 High stars, gorgeous colorful interior, great group energy.
4 The Red Parrot ⭐ 4.4 Festive & celebratory by design, 3 levels, great for big groups.
5 The Landing ⭐ 3.9 Lower stars but outdoor waterfront + live bands = peak summer bach energy.
6 Diego's ⭐ 4.3 Harbor views, margaritas, fun without being too precious.
7 Wally's Wieners ⭐ 4.3 Espresso martini towers made for this. Perfect mid-day bach stop.
8 The Reef ⭐ 4.2 Solid waterfront, good stars — more casual/generic vs. top picks.
9 O'Brien's Pub ⭐ 4.4 Great stars but just a pub — low vibe ceiling for a bach party.
10 Ma's Donuts ⭐ 4.5 Highest rated but a donut counter — grab-and-go, not an experience.
11 Smugglers Waterfront Bar ⭐ 4.0 Fun dive bar but smaller, hidden entrance, limited group options.
12 IL Forno Italiano ⭐ 3.6 Lowest stars + cash-only pizza. Late-night carb stop at best.
13 The Dockside ⭐ 3.5 Only open Fri–Sat nights, fewest reviews. Too risky to plan around.
